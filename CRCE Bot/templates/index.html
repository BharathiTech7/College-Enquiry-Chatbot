    About Us



    Rajeev Gandhi Memorial College of Engineering and Technology, Nandyal was inaugurated on 09.03.1996 by Late Sri P.V.Narasimha Rao, the former Prime Minister of India, and Sri N. Chandrababu Naidu, the former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh. This institute is one of the top engineering colleges of Andhra Pradesh. The institute has got permanent affiliation from JNTUA Anantapuramu and has been conferred Autonomous status by UGC in 2010, upgrading the curriculum and regulations as per industry and societal needs from time to time.

    All the UG eligible courses are accredited four times by NBA, recently in Tier-I category, and the institute is accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade (3.54/4.0 CGPA). It is also accredited by TCS for placements and training, recognized by DSIR for research equipment procurement, and selected for the World Bank-assisted TEQIP-1 for quality improvement. The institute is ISO 9001-2008 certified and has been awarded Best JKC Center twice by the Government of Andhra Pradesh for highest campus placements.

    RGMCET offers numerous Skill Development Programmes and has been sanctioned the Deen Dayal Upadhaya KAUSHAL Kendra under the Ministry of HRD. It was conferred the "College with Potential for Excellence" (CPE) by UGC in 2016 and recognized as a SIEMENS INC Technical Skill Development Institute (TSDI) by the Government of Andhra Pradesh in collaboration with APSSDC. The college also established two CM's Skill Centers in 2018.

    In order to make every student industry-ready, pursue higher education, and engage in research, RGMCET actively collaborates with its alumni, who frequently visit the campus to deliver expert talks and guidance. This institution is continuously working towards excellence in quality education and research.
